What to expect

  • 30 minutes, on Zoom. No deck, no pitch. We listen first.
  • Bring one pattern. The thing your team keeps doing (or not doing) that you can't quite name. The recurring meeting outcome. The retro you've had three times. The hire who's a high performer but draining the team.
  • You'll leave with a read. What behavioral mechanism is probably driving the pattern. Whether it's a fit for a workshop, a leadership-nudge program, or something else entirely.
  • If we're a fit, we'll say so. If we're not, we'll tell you that too. Either way the diagnosis is yours to keep.
